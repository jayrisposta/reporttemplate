Best Risposta Experiences

The gamification project aims to enhance customer experiences in restaurants through a satisfaction survey. Customers evaluate the service, and their responses generate customer experience (CX) indicators, which are accessed by managers and restaurant owners to guide improvements. The classification is done in rankings: Top 100, Top 50, Top 30, and Top 10, with gamification starting from the one hundredth place.

Restaurants compete against each other based on customer feedback, and as they move up the ranking, they can achieve higher categories of recognition. This system encourages the pursuit of excellence in service, creating a continuous motivation for establishments to improve their practices, always focusing on customer perceptions and feelings.
